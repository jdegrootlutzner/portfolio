In the summer of 2017, I joined the Chinook Fund as a communication and development intern...

Chinook Fund is a non-profit in Denver, Colorado supporting grassroots organizations working on issues of social and economic justice in Colorado.

As an intern, I worked on a variety of projects including designing a new monthly newsletter template, creating design pamphlets and other marketing collateral, analyzing grantee funding data, writing style guides, researching communication practices, and adding a pop-up subscription process to the website. I gained first-hand experience with the iterative design process as I created prototypes of designs that I continually adjusted with feedback from my coworkers.

Chinook Fund is an awesome organization that I am proud to have worked at. Please check out www.ChinookFund.org and subscribe to their newsletter.
